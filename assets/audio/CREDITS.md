# Audio credits

Real ambience loops used in Valhalla. Both are **CC0 / Public Domain**
(no attribution legally required — credited here as good practice).

| File       | Source sound | Author        | License | Origin |
|------------|--------------|---------------|---------|--------|
| `wind.ogg` | Wind         | felix.blume   | CC0     | via github.com/Muges/ambientsounds |
| `fire.ogg` | Fireplace    | inchadney     | CC0     | via github.com/Muges/ambientsounds |

These replace the previous procedural noise-synth ambience. Played as
looping WebAudio buffers from `js/valhalla/main.js` (`Audio.loadSamples`,
`startWind`, `startFireAmbience`).
